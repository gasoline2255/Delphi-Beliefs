What is Delphi Beliefs?

A real-time analytics dashboard for Gensyn Delphi prediction markets that tracks performance-price relationships

üéØ Overview

Delphi Beliefs is an independent analytics tool built for Gensyn Delphi, a prediction market platform where users bet on which AI models will perform best on benchmark tests. Our dashboard compares model performance data (what we call "Belief") against market prices to identify discrepancies and patterns.

ü§î What We Analyze

On Gensyn Delphi, AI models compete in benchmark tests like MMLU Pro, GPQA Diamond, and others. Market participants buy and sell shares predicting which model will win. We track whether market prices align with actual model performance to reveal interesting pricing patterns.

Performance-Price Divergence

Market prices often diverge from actual model performance data. These discrepancies reveal interesting patterns in how the market values different models relative to their benchmark results.

üìä How It Works

1. We Calculate "Belief"

As benchmark results come in, we normalize the scores to create a probability distribution. This represents what we believe each model's chance of winning should be based purely on performance data.

Belief = (Model's Score / Total of All Scores) √ó 100%

Example:

  • Grok scores 84.71 on latest eval
  • Total of all model scores: 294.38
  • Grok's Belief: 84.71 √∑ 294.38 = 28.8%

2. We Compare Against Market Price

We fetch live market prices from Gensyn Delphi and compare them to our calculated Belief percentages. The difference reveals pricing patterns and market sentiment.

3. We Categorize Price Relationships

Based on the gap between Belief and Market price, we classify each model's pricing status:

üü¢ UNDERPRICED

Belief > Market
Model's performance suggests higher value than current market price indicates.

üî¥ OVERPRICED

Market > Belief
Market price exceeds what performance data alone would suggest.

‚ö™ ALIGNED

Prices match performance
Market price closely reflects model's performance relative to competitors.

üßÆ Understanding the Data

Evals vs Belief

Evals (Evaluation Scores):

  • Raw scores from AI benchmarks (e.g., 84.71, 79.46)
  • Released by Gensyn as models complete tests
  • Shows absolute performance on specific tasks

Belief (Our Calculation):

  • Normalized percentages showing relative performance (e.g., 28.8%, 27%)
  • Represents probability of winning based on current data
  • Updates automatically as new evals are released

Market Price

The current price participants are willing to pay for shares in each model on Gensyn Delphi. Represents collective market sentiment and expectations.
